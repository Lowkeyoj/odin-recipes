Barbecue Chicken Sandwich

Description

This is how you make one of the best types of sandwiches, one that you can have either warm or cold

Ingredients Needed

  • 1/4 onion(s), small, minced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 1/2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 1/2 tbsp honey/
  • 1 tbsp cider vinegar
  • 1 1/2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 pinch salt
    • pinch pepper, freshly ground lettuce, for garnish tomato(es), sliced, for garnish onions(s), sliced,for garnish
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast(s), about 140 g
  • 1 Kaiser roll(s)

Steps Needed

  1. Pre-heat your BBQ to meduim heat
  2. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat and fry the onion and garlic. Add ketchup, honey, brown sugar, cider vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper, Cook this mixture for a few minutes to thicken the sauce. Remove from heat and pour half the sauce into a bowl. Let stand.
  3. Add the raw chicken to the bowl with the BBQ sauce and coat well. Discard this sauce.
  4. Cook chicken on the BBQ for 5-7 minutes a side or until the temperature registers 165 degrees F with a digital meat thermomet.
  5. Using the remaining BBQ to brush over your grilled chicken.
  6. Serve chicken on previously toasted Kaiser roll, topped with lettuce, tomato and onion.
